Accessibility

Accessibility in HTML provides a sense of equality. In simple words, it provides an equal access to all including the person having disabilities.
it is the practice of making web pages usuable by as many people as possible.

Why accessbility is so much essential ?

    Accessbility is much more important due to various reasons:
  • It creates an inclusive web experience for all users, including those with disabilities.
  • Accessible content is more user-friendly.
  • Accessibility skills is much more eminent to learn as it provides an helping hand to become a better developer.
  • By improving accessbility your website's SEO also can be improved.
  • it also helps in removing barriers that enabling more users to engage and participate in everyday activities.

Beneficial for different user groups

    Accessbility benefits different user groups which are as follows:
  • People with dissabilities: Accessbility provides equal access and independence to people with disabilities.
  • People with hearing loss: Subtitles and closed captioning provide text-based descriptions of audio content, allowing people with hearing loss to access media content.
  • People with low vision or reading disabilities: Accessible fonts improve legibility and readability for people with low vision or reading disabilities.
  • Usability testing: Usability testing with participants with disabilities can help identify general usability issues that might not be apparent to otherr users.
  • Coding practices: Accessbility can encourage good coding practices.
